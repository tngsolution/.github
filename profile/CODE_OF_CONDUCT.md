# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

TNG Solution is committed to providing a welcoming environment for all contributors and community members. We aim to foster an open and respectful community where individuals can collaborate effectively.

This Code of Conduct outlines the standards of behavior expected from all participants in our community.

---

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- Treat others with respect and professionalism
- Communicate clearly and constructively
- Accept constructive criticism
- Focus discussions on ideas and work
- Respect the time and contributions of others
- Acknowledge and learn from mistakes
- Honor privacy and boundaries
- Contribute positively to the community

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats
- Discrimination based on identity or characteristics
- Unwelcome advances or inappropriate comments
- Deliberate disruption of discussions
- Sharing others' personal information without consent
- Spam or commercial solicitation
- Illegal activity

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by TNG Solution, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Issue trackers and pull request comments
- Community forums and chat channels
- Organization events and meetings
- Email communications

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it.

**How to Report:**

1. **GitHub Issues** - Report through GitHub's abuse reporting system
2. **Direct Message** - Contact a maintainer or moderator privately
3. **Email** - Send a detailed report to the organization

**What to Include:**

- Description of the incident
- When and where it occurred
- Involved parties (if comfortable)
- Relevant links or documentation

Reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly. We do not tolerate retaliation against those who report violations in good faith.

---

## Investigation and Response

When a report is received:

1. It will be acknowledged and reviewed
2. An investigation will be conducted
3. Appropriate action will be taken based on findings

Possible responses include:

- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from community spaces
- Other measures as appropriate

---

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was made in error, you may submit an appeal within 30 days with a written explanation to the organization.

---

## Confidentiality

Reports are handled confidentially. Information is shared only as necessary to investigate and address violations.
